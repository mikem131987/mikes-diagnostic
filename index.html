Frequently Asked Questions

What hardware does this work with?

Mike's Diagnostic Hardware works with ThinkDiag2 VCI devices and most standard automotive diagnostic hardware. We're continuously adding support for additional devices.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All plans include a 7-day free trial with full access. No credit card required. Cancel anytime.

Can I change plans?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What if I don't provide a credit card for the trial?

The trial runs for 7 days. If you don't add a payment method, your account will expire after the trial ends.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, cancel anytime in your account settings. No penalties or hidden fees. Refunds available within 30 days of purchase.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your subscription fee, no questions asked.

What support is included?

All plans include email support. Professional and Enterprise plans include priority support with faster response times.

Can I get invoices?

Yes. Invoices are automatically generated and available in your account dashboard for download and accounting purposes.

How many users can I add?

Starter and Professional plans support 1 user. Enterprise plans include up to 10 user licenses.
